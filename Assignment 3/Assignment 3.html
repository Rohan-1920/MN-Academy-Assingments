Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is my all-time favorite food. It is a delicious rice dish made with spiced chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and fresh herbs. It is popular all over Pakistan and is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Biryani Masala
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  4. Add yogurt and biryani masala, mix well.
  5. Cook the chicken until tender.
  6. Boil the rice separately until half cooked.
  7. Layer the rice over the chicken.
  8. Cover and cook on low flame for 20 minutes.
  9. Mix gently and serve hot.

Extra Toppings and Side Items

  • Toppings
    • Fried onions
    • Fresh coriander
    • Lemon slices
  • Side Items
    • Raita (yogurt dip)
    • Salad
    • Papad
