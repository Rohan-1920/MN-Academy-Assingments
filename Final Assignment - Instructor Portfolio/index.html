Making code accessible for every learner

I am a dedicated web development instructor at MN Tech Academy, based in Karachi, Pakistan. With over five years of combined industry and teaching experience, I specialize in making complex programming concepts accessible to beginners and intermediate learners alike.

My teaching philosophy centers on hands-on learning — every lesson includes practical exercises, real projects, and constructive feedback.

I have mentored hundreds of students who have gone on to build portfolios, land internships, and start freelance careers. Before joining MN Tech Academy full-time, I worked as a front-end developer building responsive web applications for local startups.

That industry background keeps my curriculum current with modern tools and best practices. When I am not in the classroom, I contribute to open-source education resources, run weekend coding workshops in Lahore, and continuously update my skills in React, Node.js, and cloud deployment platforms.
